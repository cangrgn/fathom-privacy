Privacy Policy · Effective 6 July 2026 · WORKSJO LTD · Contact: jo@joworks.studio

English

The short version

Fathom is a local-first wellness diary. Your health entries live on your device and are never uploaded. There are no accounts. We cannot see your data.

What stays on your device (everything health-related)

  • All diary entries: stool logs (Bristol form, colour notes, symptoms), meals, hydration, stress, sleep, cycle data — stored only in a local database on your phone.
  • Bathroom photos: analysed on-device and deleted immediately by default. They are never transmitted anywhere, under any setting.
  • You can export all of your data (JSON) or permanently delete everything with one tap, at any time.

What leaves your device (two narrow cases)

  • Meal photos (optional feature): if you use "Fill from a photo", a downscaled photo of your meal is sent over an encrypted connection to our recognition endpoint solely to identify food names. The image is processed ephemerally and is not stored, logged, or used for any other purpose, including training.
  • Purchases: subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat using an anonymous identifier. We never receive your name, Apple ID, or payment details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no cloud sync of health data, no advertising, no trackers, no sale or sharing of data.
  • Optional anonymous analytics are OFF by default; even when enabled they record only that an action happened — never health values, notes, or images.

Your rights

Because your data never reaches us, access, portability, and erasure are literally in your hands: use "Export my data" and "Delete all data" in the app (Me tab). For any question, email jo@joworks.studio.

Fathom is a wellness diary. It does not diagnose, detect, or treat any condition, and it is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
